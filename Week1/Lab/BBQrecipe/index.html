Honey BBQ Chicken Wings

Follow this recipe to make amazing honey BBQ chicken wings! Great for holiday gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 20 wings
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 cup honey

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Mix flour, chili powder, salt, ground black pepper, and garlic powder together
  3. Coat the wings in flour evenly and shake off any excess
  4. Place the wings on a baking pan
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, flip the wings half way through. Remove from oven when skin is crispy and golden brown
  6. Now preheat oven to 500 degrees fahrenheit
  7. Mix the BBQ sauce and honey then coat the cooked wings in sauce
  8. Place the wings back onto the baking pan
  9. Bake for another 10 minutes, until sauce is bubbly and carmelized
